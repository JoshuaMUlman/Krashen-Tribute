Interesting Facts About Krashen

  • Stephen Krashen was born on May 14th, 1941 in Chicago, Illinois.
  • Stephen Krashen received a PhD. in Linguistics from the University of California, Los Angeles in 1972.
  • Stephen Krashen has introduced various hypotheses related to second-language acquisition, including the acquisition-learning hypothesis, the input hypothesis, the monitor hypothesis, the affective filter, and the natural order hypothesis.
  • Professor Krashen wrote his seminal work, Second Language Acquisition and Second Language Learning in 1981.
  • He is currently teaching at the University of Southern California.
  • Stephen Krashen is active both on YouTube and on his own website.
  • Professor Krashen is now posting all of his work for free on his website.
  • Krashen has published more than 486 publications in his career.
  • Stephen Krashen has been working as an activist for biligual education since at least 1998.
